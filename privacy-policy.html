Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

TopMusicianGear (topmusiciangear.com) is an independent music gear review website operated by Daniel, a professional musician based in Europe. We provide honest gear reviews and buying guides for musicians worldwide.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. When you visit our website, standard server logs may automatically record:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party tracking tools at this time.

3. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies to ensure basic functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. If you click on affiliate links, the destination retailers (Thomann, Sweetwater, Gear4Music, Plugin Boutique, Amazon, Musik Produktiv) may set their own cookies according to their respective privacy policies.

4. Affiliate Links

TopMusicianGear participates in affiliate marketing programs. When you click on a product link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps us keep the site running and creating free content for musicians.

Our affiliate partners include: Thomann, Sweetwater, Gear4Music, Plugin Boutique, and Amazon, and Musik Produktiv.

5. Third-Party Websites

Our site contains links to external retailers and partner websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before making a purchase.

6. GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights regarding your personal data including the right to access, correct, or delete any information we hold about you. Since we do not collect personal data directly, there is typically nothing to access or delete. For any questions, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: hello@topmusiciangear.com
