TopMusicianGear.com participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means that when you click on certain product links on this site and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission from the retailer — at absolutely no extra cost to you.

These commissions help us keep the website running, fund new gear purchases for testing, and continue creating free, honest content for musicians worldwide.
