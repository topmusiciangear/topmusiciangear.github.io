Mejor Software DAW para Producción Musical (2026)

Tu DAW es el corazón de tu estudio. Después de 20+ años produciendo música, los he usado todos. Estos son los DAWs que recomiendo según lo que realmente necesitas.

Cómo Elegir el Mejor DAW para Producción Musical

A DAW is the central hub of your studio — it's where you record, edit, mix, and master your music. Choosing the right DAW is a deeply personal decision.

Your genre should guide your choice. Ableton Live excels for electronic music and beat-making. Logic Pro is the best value with its massive built-in library. FL Studio has a unique pattern-based workflow for hip-hop. Cubase has the best MIDI editing. Pro Tools is the standard in professional studios.

Platform determines your options. Logic Pro is Mac-only. Most others work on both Mac and Windows. If you collaborate, choose a DAW your collaborators also use.

Try before you buy. Every major DAW offers a free trial. Download 2-3 that interest you and spend a week with each. The right DAW is the one that gets out of your way and lets you focus on music.
